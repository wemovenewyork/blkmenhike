WHO THIS IS FOR

Black men of all experience levels — never-hiked-a-day to seasoned backcountry. You don't need gear. You don't need experience. You need to be a Black man who is ready to show up for himself and for his brothers.

THE PLEDGE

When you join the chapter, you pledge one thing: no man left behind. On the trail, that's literal — you don't leave a struggling brother. Off trail, it becomes the foundation of every relationship you build here.

The Process // How You Cross

Four Steps
to Crossing.

STEP 01 // SUBMIT
Put Your Name on the Roster

Fill out the form above. Tell us where you are, where you're starting from. No experience needed. Honesty required.

Entry Point
STEP 02 // CONNECT
Meet Your Big Brother

Your chapter lead matches you with a brother who has been where you are. He answers your questions, walks you through the first steps, and brings you in right.

Big / Little
STEP 03 // SHOW UP
First Chapter Hike

You show up. You meet the line. You walk with the chapter. It doesn't matter how far or how fast — what matters is that you were there when it counted.

The Line
STEP 04 // CROSS
Your First Real Trail

The crossing is your first significant hike with the chapter — the one that tests you. When you reach the other side, you are no longer a visitor. You are a brother on the line.
