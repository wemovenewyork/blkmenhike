01 // DIRECTIVE

MEN DON'T
TALK. THE
TRAIL MAKES
THEM.

Something happens between men in the wilderness that doesn't happen anywhere else. The weight Black men carry — the code-switching, the performance of being fine, the exhaustion of constant navigation — has nowhere to go in most of their lives. The trail gives it somewhere to go. BLK MEN HIKE is built around that moment. Not the gear, not the miles. The shift that happens when Black men share difficult terrain and stop pretending.
